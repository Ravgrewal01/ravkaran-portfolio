About me

From charcoal
to Figma

I'm Ravkaran — a UI/UX designer who believes good design should feel invisible. I design interfaces that solve real problems, guided by research and refined through iteration. From charcoal sketches to polished Figma prototypes, design is how I think.

Who I am

Designer, illustrator, problem solver.

I'm a UI/UX designer currently completing my Post-Degree Diploma in Web and Mobile Design & Development (WMDD) at Langara College, Vancouver. Before moving to Canada, I completed my Master's in Computer Applications in India giving me a strong technical foundation that shapes how I approach design systems and developer handoff.

Design for me isn't just digital. I've been sketching with charcoal since long before I opened Figma and that practice of slowing down, observing, and drawing what I see directly informs how I look at UI: with patience, precision, and an eye for what really matters.
