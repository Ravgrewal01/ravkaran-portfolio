Overview

One app. Every aspect of your pet's care.

PetCare+ is a comprehensive pet health management platform built for busy owners who struggle to stay on top of their pet's wellness. It centralises health tracking, vet appointments, diet planning, and AI-powered reporting — turning fragmented pet care into a single, seamless experience.

The Problem

Pet owners are flying blind on their pet's health.

Busy pet owners struggle to consistently track their pet's health, leading to missed vaccinations, forgotten appointments, and inconsistent diet management. The information is scattered — across sticky notes, vet receipts, and memory — with no single place to get a clear picture of their pet's wellbeing.

The complexity of managing multiple responsibilities — scheduling vet visits, monitoring dietary supplements, tracking allergies, and staying on top of health alerts — creates real stress and often results in gaps in care that affect the animal's quality of life.

The Solution

Four features that unify pet care.

PetCare+ brings everything together in one intelligent dashboard. Pet Health gives owners a full medical record — vaccinations, allergies, vitals, and uploaded documents — all in one place. Appointments lets users book vets and track upcoming visits with a single tap. Diet & Supplements generates AI-powered meal plans tailored to the pet's breed, age, and health status, with completion tracking built in.

Reports & AI Chat ties it all together — giving owners instant answers to health questions and surfacing insights from their pet's data over time. The goal was to remove every friction point between an owner's good intentions and their pet actually receiving consistent, high-quality care.

Key Screens

A closer look at the experience.

01

Dashboard

The dashboard gives owners an at-a-glance view of everything that matters — pet health status, upcoming appointments, vaccination reminders, meal and nutrition data, and active alerts. A persistent sidebar keeps all five modules accessible at all times, and the "Add Pet" button makes onboarding new animals immediate.

Health Summary Alerts Panel Nutrition Chart
02

Pet Health Records

The Pet Health screen allows owners to log and upload medical documents, record vaccinations with dates and locations, track allergies and symptoms, and monitor vitals like weight, heart rate, and blood pressure. A drag-and-drop upload area supports PDF and DOC files, making it easy to digitise existing vet records.

Medical Records Vaccination Log Allergy Tracker
03

Appointments

The appointments module lets owners book with specific vets, log pet details for each visit, and choose appointment types. A structured form captures pet type, breed, age, colour, and reason for the visit — ensuring vets have everything they need before the appointment, and owners always have a record of past care.

Vet Booking Appointment Types Visit History
04

Diet & Supplements

The diet module generates AI-powered daily meal plans — breakfast, lunch, and dinner with calorie counts tailored to the pet's profile. Each meal has a "Refresh" option for variety, and a completion checkbox keeps owners accountable. Supplements like Omega-3 and Fish Oil are tracked separately, with a full nutrition summary available in the completion history tab.

Reflection

What this project taught me.

PetCare+ challenged me to design across two form factors simultaneously — a desktop web dashboard and a companion mobile app — keeping the experience coherent while adapting layouts to each context. The biggest design challenge was information density: the dashboard carries a lot of data, and every hierarchy decision directly affects how quickly a tired owner can spot what needs attention.

The teal-and-mint palette wasn't just a branding choice — it was a deliberate signal of trust, cleanliness, and care that users would associate with the medical context of the product. It communicated professionalism without feeling cold.
